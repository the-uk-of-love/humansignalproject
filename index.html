Why The Human Signal Project Exists

The Human Signal Project was created to explore one of the most profound and controversial questions of our time: are emerging nano-telecommunication technologies being detected in human bodies, what are their potential implications, and what can individuals do to investigate and respond to this phenomenon?

Our mission is to support independent research, open dialogue, practical testing, and collaborative exploration of these questions.

Our Three Pillars

Science

Independent research, evidence gathering, citizen science, and collaborative exploration.

Consciousness

Protecting the sovereignty of human consciousness by investigating the potential effects of emerging technologies on human development, while exploring natural methods for awakening and expanding human capacities without technological intervention.

Action

Transforming knowledge into practical solutions, community initiatives, and real-world impact.

Featured Research & Exploration

UK Semiconductor Detection Study

Independent UK research investigating semiconductor-related signals detected during human testing and their reduction following intervention.

Jesse Beltran's Testing Methodology

Ongoing practical testing using non-linear junction detection technology for identifying semiconductor-like responses in human subjects.

Global Human Signal Research Initiative

A vision for future international collaboration aimed at expanding independent research, standardizing methodologies, and conducting larger-scale scientific studies.
